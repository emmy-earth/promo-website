Pricing

CO2 scope 1&2 module

  • No implementation fee
  • 100% variable price: 15€/month per utility per site

Calculation example:

Your company has 2 production sites + 1 headquarter.

  • Production site 1: consumes natural gas and electricity -> 2 utilities in total
  • Production site 2: consumes natural gas, electricity, steam, and compressed air -> 4 utilities in total
  • Headquarter: consumes natural gas and electricity -> 2 utilities in total

In total: 8 utilities to manage by Emmy, resulting in a total price of 120 €/month

Other sources of CO2 (e.g., company cars and cooling liquid leaks) are also priced at 15€/month
